Open Source Developers Community

WeDev is a community for technophiles who like technology and for anyone who wants to support Open Source. We aim to promote collective intelligence and Open Source projects among students and other contributors in tech. We are a group of enthusiastic people ranging from students to professional experts willing to teach and make people thrive in Open Source.
